Space is awesome

The title says it all, space is pretty awesome. From the birth of stars to the mysteries of black holes, space offers endless fascination and awe. Here are some cool facts that showcase just how awesome space truly is.

Some random space facts.

Black holes can grow by accreting mass from their surroundings or merging with other black holes. The largest known supermassive black holes are billions of times the mass of our Sun.

The observable universe is about 93 billion light-years in diameter. This means light from the farthest objects we can see has traveled for 93 billion years to reach us!

Neutron stars are the remnants of supernova explosions and are incredibly dense. A sugar-cube-sized amount of neutron-star material would weigh about 100 million tons on Earth!

The Hercules-Corona Borealis Great Wall is the largest known structure in the universe. It's a massive galactic filament stretching over 10 billion light-years. Its sheer size is difficult to grasp.
